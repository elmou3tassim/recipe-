chekhchoukha


description

Chekhchoukha is a traditional Algerian dish from eastern Algeria (like Biskra & Setif). It features torn pieces of thin, cooked flatbread (rougag) mixed with a rich, spicy meat and vegetable sauce.

ingrdiants

for the rougag(flatbread):

    2 cups semolina Water (to form a dough) Pinch of salt

    for the sauce

  • 500g lamb or beef
    • 2 onions, chopped 2 tomatoes, grated Chickpeas (optional) Garlic, minced Spices: paprika, ras el hanout, black pepper, salt Olive oil

steps

  1. Make the rougag:
    1. Mix semolina, salt, and water to form a soft dough.
    2. Divide into balls, roll into thin rounds, cook on a hot plate or pan without oil.
    3. Tear the cooked flatbread into small pieces.
  2. Make the sauce:
    1. In a pot, saut√© onions & garlic in oil.
    2. Add meat, spices, chickpeas, and tomatoes.
    3. Cover with water and cook until meat is tender.
  3. Assemble:
    1. Place the torn rougag pieces in a large dish, pour the hot sauce over, mix well, and let it soak.
