couscous

description

Couscous is a traditional North African dish made from steamed semolina wheat granules. Itâ€™s light, fluffy, and often served with vegetables, meat, or broth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Boil water with salt and olive oil or butter.
  2. Add couscous, stir quickly, then cover and remove from heat.
  3. Let it sit for 5 minutes to absorb the water.
  4. Fluff with a fork before serving.
